About Me

I am a senior scientist with over 5 combined years of industry experience in natural products chemistry, analytical chemistry, and protein engineering for the production and commercialization of bioactive molecules.

Key Skills

Natural Product Purification and Dereplication

  • Complete structural characterization of more than 50 PKS, mixed PKS-NRPS, terpenoid, and steroidal natural products using LC-MS and NMR-based techniques
  • Natural product purification on sub-milligram to multi-gram scales using Analytical-, preparative-, and flash chromatography
  • Experience handling air- and water-sensitive compounds

Analytical Method Development

  • Experience developing methods for reverse phase, normal phase, HILIC, HyperCarb, Phenyl hexyl, and chiral stationary phases
  • Absolute quantitation using stable isotope-labelled internal standards
  • RapidFire screening methods developed for primary SSM library screening
  • Targeted and untargeted methods developed for QQQ, QToF, and OrbiTrap mass specs
  • Quantitative NMR methods developed to measure reaction kinetics, impurity analysis

Protein Engineering

  • Engineered bacterial flavin-dependent berberine bridge enzyme-like (BBE-like) enzymes for chemoselective production of cannabinoids in E. coli
  • Identified 10 additional prokaryotic BBE-like enzymes with cannabinoid synthase-like activity
  • Created SSM libraries to screen for chemoselective production of THCA, CBDA, and CBCA in bacteria
